Marijuana Legalization: Cannabis Affects Ability To Process Emotion, New Study Says

Marijuana can alter the way one processes their emotions, according to a new study. Colorado State University researchers found that pot can affect users' ability to recognize, process and "empathize with human emotions." The study, published Monday in the journal PLOS ONE, looked at the brain activities of 70 participants over the course of two years.
